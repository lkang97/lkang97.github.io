Portfolio

Codelet is a helpful study tool inspired by Quizlet. Create sets on any coding related topics you want to study and add flashcards, or choose from the many pre-made sets available. Cement your knowledge with helpful study tools such as quizzes, matching games, and flip - to - study versions of cards.

FoodFeed is a photo sharing application for food enthusiasts, inspired by Instagram. Users can create posts, upload images, comment and like each other's posts.

Clack is a messaging application inspired by Slack that brings the team together, wherever you are with all of your communication and tools in one place. Remote teams will stay productive no matter where you're working from.
